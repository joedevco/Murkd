# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 4, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Murkd ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Murkd is an anonymous confession platform where users can share thoughts and feelings publicly. Posts automatically expire and are permanently deleted after 48 hours.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly

- **Email address** — required for account creation and authentication
- **Username (ghost tag)** — a randomly generated anonymous identifier displayed on your posts
- **Password** — stored securely for authentication
- **Post content** — the text you submit as confessions

### Sensitive Information

Because Murkd is an open confession platform, your posts may voluntarily contain sensitive information such as:
- Health or mental health information (including our "MENTAL HEALTH" tag category)
- Information about your sex life or sexual orientation
- Information revealing race or ethnic origin
- Political opinions
- Religious or philosophical beliefs

We do not solicit or require this information. Any sensitive data you include is entirely voluntary.

### Information Collected Automatically

We do not use analytics services, cookies, tracking scripts, or any automatic data collection tools. We do not collect:
- Device information
- Geolocation
- Usage patterns
- IP addresses (beyond what is necessary for basic network communication)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Create and manage your account
- Enable you to post, like, and interact with confessions
- Display your anonymous ghost tag on posts
- Send push notifications (if enabled)
- Respond to your support inquiries
- Protect our service from abuse and comply with legal obligations

## 4. Push Notifications

With your permission, we may send push notifications when new confessions are posted or when someone interacts with your posts. You can opt out at any time through your device settings.

## 5. Data Retention

- **Account information** — retained as long as your account is active
- **Confessions** — automatically and permanently deleted 48 hours after posting
- **Likes and handshake reactions** — deleted when the associated post expires
- **Account deletion** — when you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed

## 6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

We use **Supabase** as our data hosting provider. Supabase acts as a data processor and processes data solely on our behalf and under our instructions.

We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests.

## 7. Age Requirement

Murkd is strictly for users 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If we discover that a user under 18 has created an account, we will delete the account and all associated data immediately.

## 8. Your Rights and Choices

- **Access and update** — you can view your account information in the Settings screen
- **Delete your account** — you can request account deletion through the Settings screen
- **Sign out** — you can sign out of your account at any time
- **Notification preferences** — you can enable or disable push notifications in your device settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.

## 9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS) and secure password hashing. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

## 10. Third-Party Services

Murkd does not use:
- Third-party analytics
- Advertising networks
- Social media integrations
- Payment processors
- Cookies or tracking technologies

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app, and if the changes are material, we will notify you via email or a prominent in-app notice.

## 12. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to exercise your data rights, contact us at:
